Service Type: All

Service Name  "Loving Me" - LGBTQIA - Domestic Abuse

Organisation:  The Emily Davison Centre

Description:

A service which provides one-to-one support and advocacy, for Trans and Non-Binary victims of domestic abuse or sexual violence who are aged 18 and over and live in England.Loving me is delivered by The Emily Davison CentreIn 2022 The EDC received funding from the Ministry of Justice and Comic Relief to provide online support to Trans and Non Binary victim of domestic abuse living in England. For more information please visit the website at www.lovingme.ukThey provide high quality support by trained specialists who understand the specific needs of the trans community as well as abuse and are a by-and-for service. The service is user-led which means that we provide support based on what the individual would like or needs.They define domestic abuse as violence, abuse or controlling behaviour perpetrated by a partner, ex-partner or family member. This can be physical, emotional, financial or sexual. We also support people affected by so called ‘honour’-based violence and forced marriage.Please Get In Touch Using One Of The Ways BelowYou can fill in the referral form found on the webpage - https://www.lovingme.uk/need-help/, email us, give us a call on the helpline or just use the live chat function.
